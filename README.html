HR Data Cleaning & Analysis – SQL Project

Project Overview

This project involved data cleaning, preprocessing, and analysis of HR datasets using MySQL to run SQL queries, and Power BI to visualize results and extract actionable insights. The goal was to understand employee demographics, tenure, turnover, and departmental performance.

Key Contributions

  • Data Cleaning & Preprocessing: Corrected inconsistent column names, standardized date formats, handled missing values, and calculated new fields (e.g., employee age).
  • Data Analysis: Explored employee demographics, tenure, job distribution, gender and race breakdowns, department performance, turnover rates, and hiring trends using MySQL queries.
  • Insights Generation: Identified trends and actionable insights through Power BI visualizations.

HR Database Cleaning Steps

  • Fixed column naming inconsistencies
  • Standardized date formats for birthdate, hire_date, and termdate
  • Calculated employee ages
  • Handled missing or null values to ensure data integrity

Insights from Data Analysis & Power BI Visuals (Click to Expand)
  • There are more male employees than female employees.
  • Race/Ethnicity: White employees are the most dominant; Native Hawaiian and American Indian employees are the least represented.
  • Age Distribution: Employees range from 23 to 60 years old. Majority are 25-34, followed by 35-44; smallest group is 55-64.
  • Location: Most employees work at headquarters compared to remote locations.
  • Average Length of Employment: Terminated employees averaged ~8 years.
  • Gender Distribution Across Departments: Generally balanced, males slightly outnumber females.
  • Department Turnover: Marketing highest, followed by Training; Research & Development, Support, Legal lowest.
  • Employee Distribution by State: Ohio has the most employees.
  • Employee Count Over Time: Net employee count increased, showing company growth.
  • Tenure Distribution by Department: Average ~8 years; Legal & Auditing highest, Services, Sales & Marketing lowest.
